### Agile Chuck Wagon, season 8, episode 9

In this episode, Chuck reviews Kent Beck's 4 Laws of Simple Design. They are that software code: 

*   Passes the tests
*   Reveals intention 
*   Has no duplication
*   Uses the fewest elements (length: 10 min)
